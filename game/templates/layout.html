Stock Jumper

Can you make it through a trading day on the Nasdaq 100?
Jump up the stocks for points or sit on a rising stock for both cash and accelerated points.
Don't fall down though! Hit the bottom of the screen and you'll die!

Stock movements are driven by real stock market data from iextrading.com for the last full trading day.


Directions
Computer users play with arrow keys.
Mobile users tap in centre to jump and tap sides to move left or right.
